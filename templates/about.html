My Story

Welcome! I'm Evan Zona, a graduate student pursuing my Master of Science in Information Systems at Indiana University's Kelley School of Business. With experience in technology consulting, AI implementation, and strategic analysis for Fortune 500 clients, I'm passionate about leveraging technology to drive innovation and create meaningful business impact.

Background

Originally from Barrington, Illinois, my academic journey led me to Indiana University where I completed my Bachelor of Science in Informatics with a Business Minor in 2025, and am currently pursuing my Master of Science in Information Systems (expected 2026). I've been recognized as a Hutton Honors College student, Founders Scholar, Shoemaker Scholar, and have achieved Dean's List recognition.

Professional Experience

Through internships at leading organizations like Crowe (Technology Transformation), NielsenIQ (Consulting Analysis), and Lovi.AI (Technology Startup), I've gained hands-on experience in AI implementation, strategic consulting, and technology solutions. I've led development teams, consulted for Fortune 500 clients, and designed innovative solutions that improve business processes.

Professional Interests

  • Innovation: Creating solutions that transform traditional business processes

Goals & Aspirations

As I complete my Master of Science in Information Systems at Indiana University, I'm focused on advancing my career in technology consulting and AI implementation.
